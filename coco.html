All About Coco

Ah yes. The real reason you're here. My adorable dog.

History

Coco was born in Arizona (or possibly Arkansas, we're still not sure) in 2012. He's eight years old now. He was adopted by my family when he was four months old. To this day, my dad and I argue over who came up with his name (it was definitely me).

Fun Facts:

  • Coco was never trained to play fetch. He just figured it out on his own.
  • Coco only knows one trick, give paw.
  • He has a longer snout than the average pomeranian. Here's some others for comparison.
  • He is agressive toward large dogs.
  • He can be very bossy.
